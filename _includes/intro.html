Project site explaining the work surrounding and comprising LOFI, a music and activity based music recommendation system leveraging wearables, smartphones and modern computing power to help relieve mental and physical stress by recommending users appropriate music.



The LOFI project was designed and executed by Juan Carlo Rebanal and Jeannie Hur for the ECE M202A course in the Winter 2019 quarter at UCLA under Dr. Mani Srivastava. We thank him for his guidance and knowledge throughout this project.
